# Executive Summary Deck Outline

Use this as the slide by slide script for a five to seven minute walkthrough of the project, in an interview or a portfolio review. Each heading below is one slide.

**Slide 1, Title.** Business Strategy and Market Share Performance Dashboard. Subtitle: An end to end SQL and Power BI engagement.

**Slide 2, The Business Problem.** Inconsistent market share reporting across business units. No clear whitespace view. No structured framework for where to invest. Pull directly from Business_Problem.md.

**Slide 3, Approach.** Five stage pipeline: raw extract, SQL cleaning, star schema transformation, KPI and BCG matrix analysis, Power BI strategy dashboard. Show the architecture diagram from the README.

**Slide 4, Data Model.** Show the ER diagram. Call out the star schema: two fact tables, four dimension tables, five years of quarterly data.

**Slide 5, Key Finding 1.** The BCG growth share matrix, built entirely in SQL. Show the Star, Cash Cow, Question Mark, Dog classification.

**Slide 6, Key Finding 2.** The whitespace opportunity funnel narrowing 150 plus region and category combinations down to the top 10 expansion targets.

**Slide 7, Key Finding 3.** Competitive leadership retention, showing where market leadership is more fragile than internal teams assume.

**Slide 8, The Dashboard.** Walk through the Executive Summary and Portfolio Analytics (BCG Matrix) pages. Demonstrate the What If reallocation slider live in Power BI, or reference the wireframe if presenting statically.

**Slide 9, Recommendations and Action Plan.** Four actions: reallocate investment from Dog to Question Mark categories, protect Cash Cow investment floor, run competitive response readiness reviews, formalize a quarterly portfolio review cadence.

**Slide 10, Impact.** Framing the investment reallocation decision in terms of the size of the whitespace opportunity identified.

**Slide 11, Technical Skills Demonstrated.** CTEs, window functions, ranking, stored procedures, views, temp tables, star schema modeling, DAX, RLS, forecasting, bookmarks, drill through, a BCG matrix built with pure SQL logic.

**Slide 12, Closing.** Every classification and ranking in this deck traces back to a reusable SQL query in the repository, not a one time spreadsheet calculation.
